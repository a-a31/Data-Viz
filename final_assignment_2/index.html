FIFA 22 Rating Analysis

With the release of FIFA 23 recently and also the FIFA World Cup in a months time, I have made a data visualisation on FIFA 22. The following data visualisations display a few analysis of players, their attributes including their overall ratings, their value, their country of origin and also exploring their potential.

Experience

As a player gets better over seasons, their rating increases. This also means their value increases. Given in Euros, the following scatterplot shows the correlation between a player's rating and their value. Note that AM stands for attacking-midfielder, CB for centre back (defenders), CDM for defending-midfielder , GK for goalkeeper, MID for midfielder and ST for striker.
